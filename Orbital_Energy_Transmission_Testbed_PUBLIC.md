# Orbital Energy Transmission Testbed (Public Concept)

## Overview
This module defines the foundational layer required before any deep-space energy network (such as SENN) becomes viable.

SENN is not the starting point.

The real starting point is:
> The ability to generate, control, and transmit energy in space.

---

## Core Idea

Before building a space energy network, humanity must first prove:

- Energy can be transmitted from orbit to Earth
- Energy beams can remain stable over distance
- Targeting and tracking systems are reliable

---

## System Components

1. Orbital Solar Power Stations  
2. Energy Transmission Layer (Laser / Microwave)  
3. Ground Receiving Stations  
4. Beam Tracking & Control Systems  

---

## Why This Matters

Without solving energy transmission:

- Deep space propulsion remains theoretical
- Node-based energy networks cannot function
- Beam divergence remains untested at scale

---

## Development Phases

Phase 1: Earth Orbit Testing  
Phase 2: Beam Control Validation  
Phase 3: Short-range Space Transmission  
Phase 4: Expansion to Deep Space Systems  

---

## Key Statement

> SENN begins only after energy transmission in space becomes controllable.
