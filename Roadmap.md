# SENN Roadmap

---

## Phase 0 – Concept Validation (0–10 years)

### Goals:
- Demonstrate node-to-node energy transfer
- Validate small-scale propulsion support

### Setup:
- Low Earth Orbit nodes
- CubeSat-scale experiments

---

## Phase 1 – Cislunar Network (10–30 years)

### Goals:
- Establish Earth–Moon SENN
- Deploy nodes at Lagrange points

### Capabilities:
- Support lunar missions
- Provide energy relay between Earth and Moon

---

## Phase 2 – Inner Solar System Expansion (30–80 years)

### Goals:
- Extend network to Mars and asteroid belt

### Capabilities:
- Support cargo transport
- Enable resource extraction operations

---

## Phase 3 – Outer System Nodes (80–150 years)

### Goals:
- Deploy nodes beyond Jupiter

### Capabilities:
- Nuclear-powered nodes
- Long-distance relay chains

---

## Phase 4 – Interstellar Precursor (150+ years)

### Goals:
- Extend SENN beyond heliosphere

### Capabilities:
- Multi-node propulsion chains
- Support high-speed probes

---

## Key Strategy

SENN must grow incrementally:

```text
LEO → Cislunar → Inner Solar → Outer Solar → Interstellar

Guiding Principle

SENN evolves through infrastructure expansion, not single breakthrough technologies.
